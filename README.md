# universal-sensor-swapper
A durable, multi-use 3D sensor swapping system with interchangeable tips. Designed for long-term sustainability and universal camera compatibility.

---

## 🧩 Terminology & Components

All design files are located in the `components/` folder. The system consists of three primary elements:

* **Base Mount (Main Body):** The core structural component that attaches to the camera. It is designed to be **scalable** to fit different device sizes.
* **Swappable Sensor Tips:** Modular attachments that connect to the Main Body, tailored for different sensor types and shapes.
* **Clamping Parts:** Specialized components designed to secure the cleaning cloth onto the tips without the need for adhesives.

---

## 🔩 Assembly & Usage

1.  **Connection:** The **Swappable Tip** is secured to the **Main Body** using a standard **M8 bolt and nut**. This ensures a rigid, durable, and long-term mechanical link.
2.  **Post-Processing:** Depending on your 3D printer's tolerances, slight **sanding** may be required on the mating surfaces to ensure a perfect mechanical fit.
3.  **Cloth Attachment:** Wrap a microfiber cloth or an eyeglass cleaning cloth over the end of the tip. Use the **clamping parts** to tighten and fix the cloth firmly in place.

---

## 🧼 Maintenance & Cleaning

* **Recommended Agent:** For professional results and sensor safety, the use of **99.9% Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA)** is highly recommended.
* **Why 99.9%?:** High-purity alcohol evaporates almost instantly and leaves **zero residue** (streaks or water spots) on the sensitive optical surface.
